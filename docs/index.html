Forecast product

Skill maps are global PNGs from the C3S verification gallery; forecasts are the region's continent embed (e.g. Africa = area11) magnified toward the country. The country's position is computed from its bbox via each map's geographic window; the sliders apply a per-region x/y nudge and zoom on top.
